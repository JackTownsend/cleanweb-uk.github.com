Thursday, September 13, 2012

7:00pm, at Forward Technology in London

Cleanweb UK are excited to announce our first Ignite evening in London, hosted by Forward Technology! Come along and see a great lineup of lightning talks all about what’s happening with sustainability and the web in the UK.

From clean clouds, to home energy, to climate visualisation, there will plenty to learn, and plenty of other attendees to get to know. It’ll be an evening to remember, so make sure you’re there!
